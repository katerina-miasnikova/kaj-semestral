This game is for 2 players sitting in front of 1 computer

Traitor

You are one of the pirates, living on the Tortuga Island. You are tired of Tortuga Master's despotia. One day pirates from Trinidad offered you big reward for your master's gold. You need to collect four barrels of gold and not get shoot.

Firstly you need to find yourself on the island. Try to do it in the way not to declassify yourself! After that you have to collect four barrels with gold. It is enough to step near it for a moment and then go away - barrel will disapper in a couple of seconds. You alse have a superpower called 'Lightning' - press Spacebar and players will be blind by a flash for a couple of seconds. You can use only once during the game.

Controls

Master

You are the Master of Tortuga. Someone said that one of your pirates ganged up with rats from Trinidad and now tries to steal your gold. Now you need to kill the traitor and everybody that noone can steel your gold!

Your aim is to find the traitor between other pirates and kill him. You have only three bullets so be careful and choose victim wisely.
